Frequently Asked Questions

What is SNIX?

SNIX is an Android app for discovering and sharing VPN configuration files. You can browse configs posted by the community, download them directly to your device, or copy them to paste into your favourite VPN app.

Is SNIX a VPN service?

No. SNIX does not provide VPN servers or connectivity itself. We are a community sharing platform for config files. The configs you find on SNIX connect to servers run by third parties — always verify a config before using it.

A downloaded config doesn't work. What do I do?

Config files shared on SNIX are contributed by community members. We do not guarantee that any config works or remains active. Try a different config, or contact the original poster. You can leave a comment on the post if the config is no longer working.

How do I delete my account?

Open the SNIX app → Profile tab → Settings → Delete Account. All your posts, comments, and profile data will be permanently removed within 30 days. This action cannot be undone.

How do I report a post or user?

Tap the ··· menu on any post or profile and select "Report". Our moderation team reviews all reports, typically within 24 hours.

Why was my post removed?

Posts may be removed for violating our Terms of Service, including sharing malicious files, spam, or content that infringes copyright. If you believe your post was removed in error, contact us at support@snix.app.

How do I report copyright infringement?

Please follow our DMCA process and send a formal notice to dmca@snix.app.

The GIF search in the banner picker isn't showing results.

The banner picker uses several public GIF APIs (Giphy, Tenor, Waifu.pics, and others). If one source isn't loading, swipe left or right on the source pills to try a different provider. Check your internet connection if all sources fail.

I found a security vulnerability.

Please report security issues responsibly to security@snix.app. Do not disclose vulnerabilities publicly until we've had a chance to address them.

Still stuck?

Email us at support@snix.app and we'll help you out.
