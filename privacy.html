🔒 Privacy Policy

Your Privacy Matters

We are committed to protecting your personal information and being transparent about what we collect.

Last updated: July 7, 2025 · Effective immediately

1Introduction

SNIX ("we", "our", or "us") operates the SNIX mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our App. Please read it carefully. By using SNIX, you consent to the practices described here.

If you do not agree with the terms of this Privacy Policy, please do not access or use the App.

2Information We Collect

Account Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Display name and profile picture (set by you)
  • Email address (via Google or Apple Sign-In)
  • Firebase UID (an anonymous identifier assigned at sign-up)

Content You Post

Any VPN configuration files, titles, descriptions, comments, or reactions you create are stored on our servers and may be visible to other users depending on your sharing settings.

Usage Data

We collect aggregate statistics such as view counts, download counts, and copy counts on posts. For guests, we track these per-device using a randomly generated identifier stored in your device's local storage — this identifier contains no personal information.

Device Information

We may collect your device type, operating system version, and app version to diagnose issues and improve performance. We do not collect precise location data.

Push Notification Tokens

If you opt in to notifications, we store your device's FCM (Firebase Cloud Messaging) token to deliver push notifications. You can revoke this at any time in your device settings.

3How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and operate the App and its features
  • To personalise your experience (e.g., recommended content)
  • To send notifications you have opted into
  • To monitor and improve App performance and security
  • To enforce our Terms of Service and Community Guidelines
  • To respond to support requests and legal obligations

We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

4Third-Party Services

SNIX uses the following third-party services that may collect data as described in their own privacy policies:

  • Firebase / Google — Authentication, Firestore database, Cloud Messaging. Google Privacy Policy
  • GIPHY / Tenor — GIF search in the profile banner picker. These services may log search queries per their own policies.
  • waifu.pics / nekos.best — Anime image sources used in the banner picker. No personal data is sent to these services.

5Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, your profile, posts, and associated data are permanently removed within 30 days, except where we are legally required to retain it longer.

Guest device identifiers stored in local storage are not synced to our servers and are erased when you clear your app data.

6Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Object to or restrict certain processing
  • Data portability (export your content)

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at mkdev12@gmail.com.

7Children's Privacy

SNIX is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately and we will delete it.

8Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including TLS encryption in transit and Firebase Security Rules to protect data at rest. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
